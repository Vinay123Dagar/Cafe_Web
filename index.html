We’re a Dedicated Indian Café.

Caféteria Delhi, named after capital of India (New Delhi). The classic casual dine Cafe started many years ago and still maintains the same quality and customer service. Caféteria Delhi is the result of research and Food trials across regional India. Our chef team continue to source information and ingredients from local cooks from different regions of India.
